Application Status

After carefully reviewing your application, we've found that we're unable to approve it at this time because there is not enough public information regarding the product, company and team/founders.

For eligibility, our program requires a globally available website that clearly showcases a digital-native business model, identifying your company solely as a tech startup offering its own app, tool, or platform. You can find all our application requirements in our FAQs here: Google for Startups Cloud Program FAQ .

This means we need to see clear details about your:

Business Description: What you do, problems you solve, and target audience.

Team Information: Key members and relevant experience.

Products: What you're building/offering and its current development stage, with images/videos/demos/link to app etc.

All of this information must be visible and easily accessible on your public website for us to determine eligibility and approve your application.

Once your website is updated with these details, please feel free to reply to this email, and we'll be happy to re-evaluate your application!

Note for stealth mode companies: If you are in stealth mode but have VC equity funding and/or can provide a Point of Contact (POC), please let us know! This information, along with investor details, can help us validate your startup for the program.

We hope this guidance helps and let us know if you need anything else!
